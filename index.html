What makes our Shop special?

EJeDominicano!

We are a small, local dominican souvenir shop in the zona colonial, Santo Domingo. We wish to make our products more accessible to people willing to learn and immerse themselves in our culture with our new online store. We sell products related to all parts of our history, from our ancestors the Tainos to today’s baseball leagues.
